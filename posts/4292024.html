Got Another In Under A Month!

APRIL 29 2024 | 3:32 AM


Not a lot to post this time, just wanted to write up a little something to stay in the habit after an almost month long break.

Feeling pretty good about my progress so far, I'm touching up a lot of my old pages, building better habits, using LinkedIn more (if you're here from there, welcome! I'm improving the site I promise), and I generally feel like I'm on the right track.

Printed out all of those thank-you cards from last time, pretty happy with the print for having no specialized equipment for it. A bit blurry around the edges in spots, but I don't think I'm getting much better out of a commercial inkjet printer. I'm half-considering just throwing the ten bucks I blew on cardstock to the wind and taking it to the UPS store for a professional print to see if it's any better, but with the twins coming soon, I don't think there's much time or money for that kind of experiment at the moment.

If I manage to land a new job sometime here soon, hey, maybe then. I'm kind of excited about the idea of running that comparison, if I'm being honest.

Nerdiest thing I've said on here so far but it's true!
